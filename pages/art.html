Album Cover Design:


Sumarry:

This is my final album cover design! It took me about an hour to get the layout figured out, but overall, I'm happy with it.
My cover is a black and white face with New Day on each side. It represents me because well, 1. Its literally my face.
2. It has NEW DAY on the sides of it, Day is my nickname, New Day is the Danish translation from Dagny, Slovak origins, and I am 25% Slovak.
3. It has a static filter (Thanks to photoshop) Which represents me because I think It looks cool, and I love older movies/shows. It reminds me of them.
I think this cover looks pretty simple, but I worked really hard on figuring out the layout and how to edit IN photoshop. (And I learned a lot too hehe)
I think If I could do something different, I would have experimented with more font types, but It only had a couple preset ones so I worked with what I had.
Besides that, I think everything was successful!
